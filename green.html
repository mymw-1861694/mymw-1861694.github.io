Green Range

Be the green

Be the green you want to see in the world. Embued with seeds or a sapling of your choosing, a plant will grow from your remains. Fully dependent on your nurturing aid, this plant will reach full maturity and start a green revolution from where you lie. It only takes one plant to start a forest.

All of us at Life After Death will ensure that your tree grows strong and healthy in your desired location. You can also assign any of our staff to be the permanent caretaker, ensuring privacy and continuity. If you would prefer no human to disturb your rest, we will send drones to drop off any supplementary nutrients and keep the plants free of pests and illness. Our company believes in full care from beginning to end. As such, we will guarantee that we will serve you well from beginning to end.
