Eco-Friendly Funeral

The latest range

With a variety of choices to choose from, the classic burial is still a popular choice. Unlike cremation, which uses up a lot of energy and produces tons of carbon dioxide, the classic burial doesn't involve desecration of the body and the environment. Encased in a biodegradable coffin, you will be helping the lifecycle of the ecosystem progress.

The Minimal Range

Spring from the Ashes

The Green Range

Worried about the rate of deforestation? Help us out by becoming a tree yourself! Choose any existing tree and we will import a sapling that can be grown from your remains. Your tree will be protected due to it also being your grave. Foster a new life and guide it to a better tomorrow.

About Us

The Origin Story

Started by a company of eco-conscious teeagers, we pondered the imminency of death. And we came to the comclusion that we may not be able to decide how we go out, but we can decide what we want to do with our bodies when we do. As such, we came up with this concept of returning to the world that let us grow, contributing to the cycle of life.
